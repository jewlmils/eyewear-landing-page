What materials are AKILA sunglasses made from?

AKILA eyewear is made from plant-based cellulose acetate, an eco-conscious alternative to cheaper plastics that allows for a wide range of unique colors and patterns. All frames are completed with sturdy barrel hinges and stainless steel temple cores for durability, then fitted with optical class 1 nylon lenses for near-perfect clarity and scratch-resistance.

Is the fit of AKILA sunglasses adjustable?

The cellulose acetate AKILA sunglasses are made from is a malleable material that can be gently modified and gradually shaped for your specific size and use. The arms of all AKILA frames have a metallic core that holds the acetate in place and can be adjusted easily to fit your face shape.

How should I clean my AKILA eyewear?

All sunglasses should be frequently cleaned and maintained to preserve the original design and functionality. Your AKILA eyewear is best cleaned under running water, then dried with the soft cloth included in your purchase.
